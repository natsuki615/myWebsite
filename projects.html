By Tooth And Beak
Interactive visualization of bird diet using UMAP dimension-reduction and grid projection(rasterization). Hovering and clicking every bird speicies triggers animations and a description panel.
Bubbly
Real-time body tracking that identifies and classifies ASL alphabet gestures. Made using TensorFlow.js and p5.js, this program paints the letters detected with a bubbly effect. Click to see a demo video.
chomp chomp!
Dive into the wacky underwater brawl of Chomp Chomp!, a fast-paced, hand-tracking VR game where your own hands become fish puppets! Using hand-tracking technology and developed in OpenProcessing, players control fish-shaped puppets that snap their jaws shut with a simple movement. Moreover, explore other features and appearances of the fish by moving your fingers. The goal? Chomp your opponent before they chomp you! Click to launch it on itch.io.
Agaric’s Curse
A 2D single-player adventure game where the player navigates a hallucinatory cave to find the exit. Along the way, the player will encounter several flying monsters trying to stop the player from escaping and to kill them—so that the monsters can consume the player for energy.
the weather within me
A wallpaper that changes dynamically based on the weather of your current location. * Using this wallpaper or entering the website requires access to your device's location. If access for the Geolocation API isn't granted, the program opts to use the device's IP address.
